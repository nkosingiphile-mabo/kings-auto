Our Mission & Vision

Our Mission

At Boss Auto Repairs, our mission is to deliver premium, reliable, and transparent automotive repair services by combining advanced diagnostics, skilled workmanship, and genuine customer care. We are committed to keeping every client safely on the road through affordable, accredited service rooted in integrity and passion for excellence.

Our Vision

To become Newcastle's most trusted and innovative auto repair partner, known for our expertise, personalized service, and unwavering commitment to quality. We aim to empower drivers with confidence by keeping their vehicles in top condition through forward-thinking solutions and expert care.

Meet “The Boss”

Gerard Andrews, affectionately referred to as “The Boss”, stands at the helm of Boss Auto Repairs — a respected establishment situated in the heart of Newcastle’s CBD.

With over 11 years of hands-on experience in motor mechanics, Gerard’s journey began with formal training under the esteemed Mercedes Benz brand, where he honed his skills to perfection.

His dedication to quality and precision led him to obtain certifications for specialized repairs on Mercedes Benz vehicles, solidifying his reputation as a trusted authority in the field.

All Makes & Models

At Boss Auto Repairs, we take pride in offering comprehensive servicing for all vehicle makes and models. Our meticulous maintenance procedures ensure your vehicle’s optimal performance and longevity.

From diagnostic scanning, oil and filter replacements, handbrake adjustments, to full safety inspections — your car is in expert hands. Gerard’s dedication to professionalism and customer satisfaction drives our commitment to deliver the highest standard of care to the Newcastle community.

Latest Diagnostic Technology

At Boss Auto Repairs, we harness the power of cutting-edge diagnostic technology to precisely identify any issues plaguing your vehicle, enabling us to undertake swift and efficient repairs. Our expertise spans a wide spectrum of automotive systems, encompassing repairs for the engine, wheels, brakes, gearboxes, clutches, cambelts, and suspensions, among others.

With access to comprehensive technical data, our skilled technicians are equipped to tackle various repair challenges—from diff repairs to master cylinders, turbo repairs, exhaust systems, shock absorbers, toothed belt replacements, wheel bearings, steering problems, and oil leaks. We are committed to delivering top-notch service and ensuring your vehicle receives meticulous attention, restoring it to optimal performance and reliability.
